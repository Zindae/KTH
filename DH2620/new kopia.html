MonkeyMonkeys are generally considered to be intelligent. Unlike apes, monkeys usually have tails.

VultureA particular characteristic of many vultures is a bald head, devoid of normal feathers.

OstrichThe ostrich is farmed around the world, particularly for its feathers, which are decorative and are also used as feather dusters.

ZebraZebras are several species of African equids (horse family) united by their distinctive black and white stripes. Their stripes come in different patterns, unique to each individual.

SnakeSnakes are elongate, legless, carnivorous reptiles of the suborder Serpentes that can be distinguished from legless lizards by their lack of eyelids and external ears.

TigerTigers are among the most recognisable and popular of the world's charismatic megafauna. They have featured prominently in ancient mythology and folklore.
